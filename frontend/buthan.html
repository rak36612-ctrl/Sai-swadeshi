BUTHAN PACKAGE 6N/7D

Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Arrival at Bagdogra – Transfer to Phuentsholing

  • Upon landing at Bagdogra International Airport, embark on a journey to Phuentsholing, the gateway to Bhutan. Situated along the northern edge of the Indian plains, this vibrant commercial town offers a blend of cultures. After checking into your hotel, enjoy a delightful dinner and unwind for the night. Meals: Lunch & Dinner
▼

Day - 2:- Phuentsholing Thimphu

  • After completing immigration procedures, proceed to Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan. Enroute, enjoy: • Kharbandi Gompa Monastery – A peaceful monastery with scenic views. • Waterfall Stop – A refreshing natural attraction along the way. • Chuzom – The picturesque confluence of the Paro and Thimphu rivers. Upon reaching Thimphu, check into your hotel, savor a delicious dinner, and rest overnight. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
▼

Day - 3:- Excursion to Punakha – Return to Thimphu

  • Start your day with breakfast before heading towards Punakha/Wangdue. On the way, pause at: • Dochu-La Pass (3,150 meters) – A panoramic viewpoint offering breathtaking Himalayan vistas. • Punakha Dzong – A historic fortress built in 1637, also known as Druk Pungthang Dechen Phodrang (Palace of Great Happiness), which serves as the winter residence of the Je Khenpo. • Hanging Bridge, Punakha – One of Bhutan’s longest suspension bridges. • Optional River Rafting – Adventure seekers can enjoy rafting in Punakha for an additional charge of ₹2000 per person. Return to Thimphu in the evening for dinner and an overnight stay. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
▼

Day - 4:-Thimphu Sightseeing – Overnight Stay in Paro

  • After breakfast, explore Thimphu’s key attractions: • Kuensel Phodrang (Buddha Dordenma) – A towering statue of Buddha with panoramic city views. • National Memorial Chorten (view from outside) – A sacred stupa built in memory of Bhutan’s third king. • Sangaygang Viewpoint – A hilltop vantage point offering stunning views of the valley. • Changangkha Monastery – A revered temple offering blessings for visitors. • Art & Craft Market – A great place to explore Bhutanese handicrafts. Later, transfer to Paro and enjoy a traditional Bhutanese folk dance around a bonfire at your hotel. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
▼

Day - 5:- Exploring Paro – Tiger’s Nest Hike

  • Start the day with a trek to Bhutan’s most famous landmark: • Tiger’s Nest Monastery (Taktsang Monastery) – Perched on a cliff, this sacred site offers breathtaking views and a spiritual experience. (Guests who prefer not to hike can enjoy shopping in Paro instead.) After lunch, continue exploring local markets in Paro before returning to your hotel for an overnight stay. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
▼

Day - 6:- Chele La Pass – Return to Phuentsholing – Night Stay in Siliguri

  • Early morning, set out for: • Chele La Pass – Bhutan’s highest motorable road, often covered in snow. After breakfast and lunch in Paro, drive back to Phuentsholing (approximately 160 km). Spend the evening shopping before heading to Siliguri for an overnight stay. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
▼

Day - 7:- Departure from Phuentsholing to Bagdogra

  • After breakfast, complete the necessary immigration formalities, check out from the hotel, and drive to Bagdogra International Airport, cherishing the beautiful memories of your Bhutan trip. Meals: Breakfast & Lunch
