RAJASTHAN PACKAGE 7N/8D

XXXXX/-

Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Bangalore to Udaipur

  • • Fly from Bangalore to Udaipur. • Upon arrival, visit Shrinath Ji Temple in Nathdwara, renowned for its striking black stone idol of Lord Krishna. • Explore the Statue of Belief, an awe-inspiring 369-foot statue of Lord Shiva, one of the world’s tallest. • Check into your hotel for an overnight stay in Udaipur.
▼

Day - 2:- Discovering Udaipur

  • • Start your day with a visit to City Palace, an architectural masterpiece offering stunning views of Lake Pichola. • Explore Jagdish Temple, an ancient Hindu temple known for its intricate carvings. • Walk through the serene gardens of Saheliyon Ki Bari, adorned with fountains, marble pavilions, and lush greenery. • In the evening, enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Lake Pichola, passing by the beautiful Jag Mandir. • Return to your hotel for an overnight stay in Udaipur.
▼

Day - 3:- Udaipur to Jodhpur

  • • Travel to Jodhpur, the famous Blue City. • Visit Jaswant Thada, an elegant marble cenotaph built in honor of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II. • Spend some time shopping for local handicrafts, textiles, and souvenirs. • Check into your hotel and relax for an overnight stay in Jodhpur.
▼

Day - 4:-Jaisalmer – The Golden City

  • • Depart for Jaisalmer, a city famous for its golden sandstone architecture. • Explore the magnificent Jaisalmer Fort, a living fort with vibrant markets and ancient temples. • Visit the intricately carved Jain Temples within the fort. • In the evening, head to Sam Sand Dunes for a memorable desert experience, featuring a camel safari, mesmerizing sunset views, and traditional Rajasthani folk performances. • Stay overnight in Jaisalmer.
▼

Day - 5:- Jaisalmer to Jodhpur

  • • Return to Jodhpur and visit the grand Umaid Bhavan Palace, an exquisite blend of Indo-Colonial and Art Deco architecture. • Explore the mighty Mehrangarh Fort, one of India’s most impressive forts, offering breathtaking views of Jodhpur’s blue houses. • Enjoy a comfortable overnight stay in Jodhpur.
▼

Day - 6:- Chele La Pass – Return to Phuentsholing – Night Stay in Siliguri

  • • Travel to Pushkar, a sacred town famous for its spiritual and cultural heritage. • Visit the revered Pushkar Lake, where devotees take a holy dip to wash away sins. • Explore the Brahma Temple, one of the few temples in the world dedicated to Lord Brahma. • Visit Rangji Temple, which features a unique mix of South Indian, Rajput, and Mughal architecture. • Overnight stay in Pushkar.
▼

Day - 7:-Pushkar to Jaipur

  • • Depart for Jaipur, the royal Pink City. • Stop at Jal Mahal, a stunning palace floating in the middle of Man Sagar Lake. • Visit the renowned Hawa Mahal, known as the Palace of Winds, featuring 953 intricately designed windows. • Explore Jantar Mantar, a UNESCO World Heritage site, housing ancient astronomical instruments. • End your day with a visit to the Birla Temple, a beautifully constructed white marble temple.
▼

Day - 8:-Ranthambore Safari

  • • Early Morning enjoy a jungle safari in Ranthambore National Park. (The Ranthambore Tiger Reserve, situated in Sawai Madhopur in the northern Indian state of Rajasthan, is one of the largest tiger habitats in the country). Return to the hotel, check-out, and drive to Jaipur for return Journey with sweet memories of the trip.
