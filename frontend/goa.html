Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Arrival in Goa

  • Upon arrival at Goa Airport Sai Swadeshi Tours will provide a warm welcome and smooth transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Begin your journey with an introduction to Goa’s rich Portuguese heritage and traditions. Visit iconic Old Goa churches, including: Basilica of Bom Jesus St. Francis Xavier Church Explore Fort Aguada, a famous Portuguese-era lighthouse offering panoramic views. Enjoy a delicious dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.
▼

Day - 2:- North Goa Exploration

  • After breakfast, set out to explore the vibrant beaches of North Goa, popular among Indian and international tourists. Experience thrilling water sports, including: Parasailing Scuba diving Water skiing Visit Anjuna, Baga, and Calangute beaches for sun, sand, and adventure. Explore the local markets in Anjuna, where you can shop for: Traditional jewelry Handicrafts Souvenirs As the sun sets, dive into Goa’s electrifying nightlife, including: Tito’s Bar Ingo’s Saturday Night Bazaar Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.
▼

Day - 3:-South Goa & Departure

  • On the final day, head towards South Goa for a more serene experience. Visit the famous Colva Beach, known for: Beach shacks Lively discos Delicious coastal eateries After a relaxing time by the sea, Sai Swadeshi Tours will assist you with a smooth transfer to Goa Airport for your onward journey, leaving you with unforgettable memories of Goa.
