Itinerary

▼

Day 1 Bangalore – Pune

  • Begin your spiritual journey with a comfortable flight from Bangalore to Pune. Upon arrival at Pune Airport, board the vehicle and proceed towards Shirdi. Enroute, visit the revered Ranjangaon Mahaganapathi Temple.
  • Once in Shirdi, you will be provided with a room to freshen up before heading to the Sai Baba Samadhi Mandir for a special VIP Darshan. Explore other significant sites such as Gurusthan, the Sai Baba Museum, Dwarakamai, and Chawadi. After seeking blessings, return to the hotel for lunch.
  • Later, proceed to Shani Shingnapur (78 km) to visit the famous temple dedicated to Lord Shani. After a fulfilling darshan, transfer back to Pune Airport for your return flight to Bangalore, carrying the divine blessings of Sai Baba.
