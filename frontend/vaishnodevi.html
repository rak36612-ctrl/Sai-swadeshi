VAISHNO DEVI PACKAGE 3N/4D

XXXX/-

Itinerary

▼

Day 1|Bangalore – Amritsar – Katra

  • Arrival at Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport, Amritsar. Sai Swadeshi Tours will provide a smooth transfer. Proceed towards Katra (260 Km | ~7 Hours). Enroute, enjoy midway lunch. Upon arrival, check in to the hotel. Dinner and overnight stay in Katra.
▼

Day 2 | Katra – Vaishno Devi

Katra – Vaishnodevi
  • After breakfast, proceed towards the sacred Mata Vaishno Devi Temple. Vaishno Devi is considered a manifestation of Maa Durga, created from the combined energies of Kali, Lakshmi, and Saraswati. Darshan and blessings at the holy shrine. Return to Katra for an overnight stay.
▼

Day 3 | Katra – Jammu – Amritsar

Katra – Jammu – Amritsar
  • After breakfast, proceed towards Jammu. Visit the Raghunath Ji Temple, one of the most revered temples dedicated to Lord Rama. Continue the journey towards Amritsar (midway lunch). Visit the India-Pakistan Wagah Border to witness the iconic retreat ceremony, performed daily before sunset. After the ceremony, proceed to Amritsar. Dinner and overnight stay in Amritsar.
▼

Day 4 | Amritsar – Bangalore

Amritsar – Bangalore
  • After breakfast, visit: Golden Temple, the holiest shrine of Sikhism, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib. Jallianwala Bagh, the historic site of the 1919 massacre. Transfer to Amritsar Airport for the return flight to Bangalore, carrying the blessings of Mata Vaishno Devi and cherished memories of the pilgrimage.
