VIETNAM PACKAGE 5N/6D

XXXX/-

Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Exploring Da Nang

  • Arrive in Da Nang in the morning and enjoy an early lunch at Benaras. Check into the Nesta Da Nang Hotel (4* Superior room) before heading out to explore the stunning Son Tra Peninsula in the afternoon. In the evening, take a mesmerizing lantern boat ride along the Hoi An River, followed by the captivating Teh Dar Show in Da Nang. End the day with a buffet dinner at Rasoi and an overnight stay at Nesta Da Nang Hotel.
▼

Day - 2:-A Day in Ba Na Hills

  • Set off for a full-day excursion to the breathtaking Ba Na Hills, a scenic mountain retreat. Enjoy a delicious lunch at Bharata before returning to Da Nang for a delightful buffet dinner at Bhojan. Spend the night in comfort at Nesta Da Nang Hotel.
▼

Day - 3:- Hoi An’s Coastal Charm

  • Start your day with beach activities and water sports in Da Nang (at your own expense), followed by a relaxing lunch in the city. Later, travel to Hoi An for a peaceful Coconut Forest Water Boat Ride. Check into the Hoian Lantana Hotel (4* Superior room) and spend the evening enjoying the Impressions Memories Show. End the night with a buffet dinner in Hoi An before resting at your hotel.
▼

Day - 4:- Discovering Hanoi

  • Begin the morning with shopping at Han Market in Da Nang before having lunch in the city. Catch an afternoon flight to Hanoi and check into the opulent Dolce Hotel Golden Lake (5* Superior room). In the evening, immerse yourself in Hanoi’s cultural scene with a traditional Water Puppet Show and a guided tour of the Old Quarter. Wrap up the day with dinner at India Gate and a relaxing overnight stay.
▼

Day - 5:-Halong Bay Cruise Adventure

  • Check out from the Hanoi hotel and embark on the luxurious Halong Bay Cruise (Amanda Luxury). Spend the day sailing through stunning landscapes, enjoying freshly prepared meals for lunch and dinner, and unwinding in your 5* Deluxe Ocean View cabin aboard the Halong Amanda Cruise.
▼

Day - 6:-Hanoi Sightseeing & Departure

  • Disembark from the cruise and return to Hanoi for a city tour, visiting iconic landmarks such as the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, One Pillar Pagoda, West Lake, and Turtle Island. Enjoy an afternoon of shopping before savoring a delightful dinner in the city. Conclude your Vietnam journey with a late-night flight back to India at 3:00 AM.
