KASHI AND AYODHYA PACKAGE 2N/3D

XXXXX

Itinerary

▼

Day 1|Bangalore – Varanasi

  • Depart from Kempegowda International Airport to Varanasi Airport by flight. Upon arrival, Sai Swadeshi Tours will provide a smooth transfer. Proceed to Sarnath, a significant Buddhist site where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon, marking the beginning of the Buddhist Sangha. After the visit, check in to your hotel and freshen up. In the evening, witness the divine Ganga Aarti at the River Ganges (option to enjoy the Aarti by boat). Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Varanasi.
▼

Day 2|Varanasi – Ayodhya

  • Early morning, proceed for VIP Darshan at Kashi Vishwanath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. Visit other sacred temples in Varanasi: Annapurna Temple Vishalakshi Temple Kalabhairava Temple Return to the hotel for breakfast, then checkout and proceed towards Ayodhya. Upon arrival, check in to the hotel. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay in Ayodhya.
▼

Day 3|Ayodhya – Lucknow – Return to Bangalore

  • After breakfast, check out and explore the sacred sites of Ayodhya, believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama: Ram Janmabhoomi, the historic birthplace of Lord Rama Hanuman Garhi, a revered temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman Ram Darbar Mandir, depicting Lord Rama, Goddess Sita, Hanuman Ji, and Lakshman Ji, symbolizing their divine bond Sarayu River, a holy river associated with Lord Rama After sightseeing, transfer to Lucknow Airport/Ayodhya Airport for your return flight to Bangalore, carrying the blessings of Lord Rama and divine memories of the journey.
