GURUVAYUR PACKAGE 1N/2D

XXXXX/-

Itinerary

▼

Day 1| Bangalore – Kochi – Guruvayur

  • Departure from Bangalore to Kochi by flight. On arrival at Kochi Airport, board the vehicle and proceed to Chottanikkara Bhagavathi Temple, dedicated to Goddess Bhagavati Lakshmi, who resides here along side Maha Vishnu. After darshan, proceed to Alleppey and board a traditional Kerala houseboat. Enjoy a delicious Kerala lunch while cruising through the scenic backwaters. In the evening, disembark from the houseboat and proceed to Guruvayur. On arrival, check in to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay in Guruvayur.
▼

Day 2| Guruvayur – Kochi – Bangalore

  • Early morning, visit the Guruvayur Temple for a VIP darshan of Sri Krishna. Return to the hotel for breakfast, then check out and proceed to Kochi. Upon arrival, visit: Fort Kochi, known for its Dutch, Portuguese, and British colonial architecture and the iconic Chinese fishing nets at Fort Kochi Beach. Mattancherry Palace & Jewish Synagogue, showcasing Kerala’s rich history and culture. Marine Drive, where you can enjoy a leisurely walk along the waterfront. Hill Palace Museum, a historical archaeological museum and former royal palace. After dinner at a local restaurant, transfer to Cochin Airport for your return journey to Bangalore, carrying divine blessings and unforgettable memories of Sri Krishna.
