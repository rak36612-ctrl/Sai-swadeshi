SRILANKA PACKAGE 6N/7D

Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Arrival & Journey to Jaffna via Mannar

  • Arrive in Colombo and meet your tour representative at the airport.
  • Begin the journey to Mannar, a scenic 5-6 hour drive.
  • Stop for lunch at an Indian restaurant along the way.
  • Visit Thirukketheeswaram Temple, one of the revered Pancha Ishwarams dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Continue onward to Jaffna for check-in and relaxation.
  • Enjoy dinner at an Indian restaurant in Jaffna before settling in for the night.
▼

Day - 2:- Exploring Jaffna

  • Start the day with breakfast at the hotel.
  • Take a boat ride to Nainativu Island and visit the Nagapooshani Amman Temple.
  • Return to Jaffna for lunch at an Indian restaurant.
  • Explore the Jaffna Library, a symbol of cultural heritage.
  • Visit the revered Nallur Kandaswami Amman Temple.
  • Discover the fascinating Nilavarai Bottomless Well, steeped in legends.
  • Stop at Keerimalai Naguleswaram Temple, a sacred Hindu site.
  • Unwind at Casuarina Beach, known for its pristine shores.
  • Conclude the day with dinner at an Indian restaurant and an overnight stay in Jaffna.
▼

Day - 3:- Journey to Trincomalee

  • Have breakfast at the hotel before departing for Trincomalee, a scenic 4-5 hour drive.
  • Arrive in Trincomalee and enjoy lunch at an Indian restaurant.
  • Visit the famous Koneswaram Temple, a revered Hindu pilgrimage site.
  • Experience the natural Kanniya Hot Water Wells, known for their healing properties.
  • Wrap up the day with dinner at an Indian restaurant and an overnight stay in Trincomalee.
▼

Day - 4:- Trincomalee to Kandy via Sigiriya

  • Start the day with breakfast at the hotel.
  • En route to Kandy, stop to climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Enjoy lunch at an Indian restaurant in Sigiriya.
  • Explore the vibrant streets of Kandy with a guided city tour.
  • Witness a traditional cultural performance featuring Sri Lankan dance and music.
  • Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic, a sacred Buddhist site.
  • Stop by a Gem Museum & Shop to learn about Sri Lanka’s famous gemstones.
  • End the day with dinner at an Indian restaurant and an overnight stay in Kandy.
▼

Day - 5:- Kandy to Nuwara Eliya – The Hill Country Experience

  • Enjoy breakfast at the hotel before departing for Nuwara Eliya.
  • Visit a Tea Plantation & Factory to learn about the art of tea-making.
  • Stop at Ramboda Falls, a breathtaking natural wonder.
  • Have lunch at an Indian restaurant in Ramboda.
  • Visit the Seetha Amman Temple, associated with the Ramayana legend.
  • Stroll through Victoria Park, a peaceful retreat in Nuwara Eliya.
  • Explore the charming Nuwara Eliya town, known for its colonial architecture.
  • Conclude the day with dinner at an Indian restaurant and an overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.
▼

Day - 6:- Nuwara Eliya to Ella & Colombo

  • Start the morning with breakfast at the hotel before heading to Ella.
  • Visit the stunning Ravana Falls, linked to the Ramayana epic.
  • Admire the Demodara Nine Arch Bridge, an engineering marvel.
  • Enjoy lunch at an Indian restaurant in Ella.
  • Travel back to Colombo for check-in and relaxation.
  • Have dinner at an Indian restaurant before an overnight stay in Colombo.
▼

Day - 7:- Colombo City Tour & Departure

  • After breakfast, embark on a Colombo city tour, visiting key attractions like Galle Face Green, Independence Square, and more.
  • Stop for lunch at an Indian restaurant.
  • Enjoy some free time for shopping at local markets and souvenir stores.
  • Have dinner at an Indian restaurant before heading to the airport.
  • Transfer to the airport for your flight home, concluding your Sri Lankan adventure.
