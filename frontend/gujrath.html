Itinerary

▼

Day 1 |Bangalore – Ahmedabad

  • Meet and greet at Kempegowda International Airport, Bangalore. Board the flight to Ahmedabad. Upon arrival at Ahmedabad Airport, board the vehicle and proceed for breakfast. After breakfast, visit Adalaj Stepwell (also known as Rudabai Stepwell), an intricately designed stepwell near Gandhinagar. Later, explore Sabarmati Ashram, the historic residence of Mahatma Gandhi. In the evening, visit Akshardham Temple in Gandhinagar. Return to Ahmedabad for dinner and an overnight stay.
▼

Day 2 |Ahmedabad – Dwaraka

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and embark on a journey to Dwarka (approximately 450 km). Enjoy lunch enroute. Upon arrival, check in to the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, visit the revered Shree Dwarkadhish Temple, one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.
▼

Day 3 |Dwaraka

  • After breakfast, take a boat ride to Bet Dwarka, an important pilgrimage site. On the return journey, visit Gopi Talav, a sacred pond associated with Lord Krishna, followed by Nageshwar Jyotirlinga (one of the twelve Jyotirlingas) and Rukmini Temple. In the evening, enjoy another darshan at Shree Dwarkadhish Temple. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Dwarka.
▼

Day 4 |Dwarka – Porbandar – Somnath

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and travel to Porbandar (approximately 100 km). Visit Kirti Mandir, the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi, and Sudama Mandir, dedicated to Sudama, the childhood friend of Lord Krishna. Continue the journey to Somnath, check in to the hotel, and freshen up. Visit Triveni Sangam, the sacred confluence of the Hiran, Kapila, and Saraswati rivers with the Arabian Sea. Explore Geeta Mandir and Bhalka Tirth, where Lord Krishna is believed to have left his mortal body. In the evening, visit the renowned Somnath Jyotirlinga Temple and enjoy the mesmerizing Light and Sound Show. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.
▼

Day 5 |Somnath – Gir Forest – Bhavnagar

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and head to Gir Forest National Park, home to the majestic Asiatic lions. Enjoy an exciting safari ride through the wilderness. After the safari, proceed to Bhavnagar, stopping for lunch along the way. Dinner and overnight stay at Bhavnagar.
▼

Day 6 |Bhavnagar – Nishkalank Mahadev – Statue of Unity

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and visit Nishkalank Mahadev Temple, a unique Shiva temple located in the middle of the sea, accessible only during low tide. This sacred site is believed to have been worshipped by the Pandavas. Later, proceed to the Statue of Unity, the world's tallest statue dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. Dinner and overnight stay at a hotel near the Statue of Unity.
▼

Day 7 | Statue of Unity

  • After Breakfast , Checkout hotel and proceed to Visit World’s Tallest Statue of Unity (Sardar Vallabhabhai Patel Bronze statue- 182m), Also Visit Valley of Flowers and Sardar Sarovar Dam. Enroute lunch, Then proceed to Ahmedabad airport for your return journey to Bangalore with the sweet memories of the tour.
