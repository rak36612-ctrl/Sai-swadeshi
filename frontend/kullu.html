Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Bangalore – Chandigarh – Manali

  • • Morning: Arrive at Chandigarh Airport and board a vehicle for your journey to Manali, a scenic hill station nestled in the Himalayas. • Afternoon: Enjoy the picturesque drive through winding mountain roads, lush valleys, and charming villages. • Evening: Arrive in Manali and check into your hotel. • Night: Enjoy a delicious dinner and relax with an overnight stay in Manali.
▼

Day - 2:- Exploring Manali

  • • Morning: After breakfast, set out for a day of sightseeing in and around Manali. • Daytime: • Head towards Rohtang Pass, passing through the stunning Solang Valley, known for its breathtaking landscapes and adventure sports. • Spend time amidst the snow-clad mountains and enjoy activities like skiing and snowboarding (subject to season and weather conditions). • Return to Manali and visit Vashisht Temple, famous for its natural hot water springs, believed to have therapeutic properties. • Evening: Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.
▼

Day - 3:- More Sightseeing in Manali

  • • Morning: Enjoy a hearty breakfast before heading towards Anjani Mahadev Temple in Solang Valley, known for its seasonal waterfall. Reach the temple by horse ride, walking, or an electric vehicle ride. • Afternoon: After lunch, visit: • Hadimba Temple, a 450-year-old shrine dedicated to Goddess Hadimba, set amidst cedar forests. • Mall Road, the bustling heart of Manali, perfect for shopping and trying local delicacies. • Evening: Return to the hotel for a relaxing dinner and an overnight stay.
▼

Day - 4:-Manali - Kullu - Chandigarh

  • • Morning: After breakfast, start your journey towards Chandigarh, with enroute visits to: • Vaishno Mata Temple in Kullu, a sacred Hindu temple nestled along the Beas River. • River Rafting & Paragliding Point, where adventure enthusiasts can opt for thrilling river rafting or paragliding experiences (optional). • Evening: Arrive in Chandigarh and check into your hotel. • Night: Dinner and overnight stay in Chandigarh.
▼

Day - 5:- Chandigarh - Bangalore

  • • Morning: After breakfast, visit The Rock Garden of Chandigarh, an extraordinary sculpture garden made from industrial and household waste, showcasing creative art installations. • Afternoon: Transfer to Chandigarh Airport for your return journey, carrying wonderful memories of your trip.
