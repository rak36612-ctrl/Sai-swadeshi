Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Arrival in Darbhanga and Journey to Janakpur

  • Upon reaching Darbhanga, proceed with a transfer to Janakpur. Explore the key attractions of Janakpur before checking into the hotel. Take some time to rest and refresh. Enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel.
▼

Day - 2:- Flight to Kathmandu & City Tour

  • After breakfast, board a flight to Kathmandu. Upon arrival, proceed directly for a city tour, visiting Pashupatinath Temple, Guhyeshwari Temple, Budhanilkantha, Swayambhunath Stupa, and Kathmandu Durbar Square. Later, transfer to the hotel for check-in and an overnight stay.
▼

Day - 3:- Explore Bhaktapur

  • After breakfast, set out for a city tour of Bhaktapur, visiting Bhaktapur Durbar Square, Saga Mahadev, and Doleshwor Mahadev. Later, return to the hotel for an overnight stay.
▼

Day - 4:- Drive to Pokhara (9 Hours)

  • Enjoy breakfast before embarking on a scenic 9-hour drive to Pokhara, covering a distance of 200 km from Kathmandu. En route, visit the famous Manakamana Temple. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Overnight stay at the hotel.
▼

Day - 5:- Journey to Muktinath & Jomsom (7-8 Hours)

  • Early in the morning, take a packed breakfast and depart for Muktinath, a 7-hour drive from Pokhara. Vehicles are not permitted near the temple, so they stop about 1.7 km away. From there, walk approximately 500 meters uphill via stairs to reach the temple for darshan. Afterward, return to Jomsom for an overnight stay.
▼

Day - 6:- Return to Pokhara (5-6 Hours)

  • After an early breakfast, begin the journey back to Pokhara, which takes approximately 5-6 hours. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and take some time to rest and refresh. Enjoy a free day in Pokhara before an overnight stay at the hotel.
▼

Day - 7:- Pokhara Sightseeing & Flight to Bhairahawa

  • Start the day with an early morning visit to Sarangkot for a breathtaking sunrise view, located just 25 minutes away. After sunrise, return to the hotel for breakfast before heading out for a sightseeing tour of Pokhara. Visit key attractions including Devi’s Fall, Gupteshwor Cave, Bindabasini Temple, Fewa Lake, the International Mountain Museum, and the Seti River. Later, transfer to the airport for a flight to Bhairahawa. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel for an overnight stay.
▼

Day - 8:- Lumbini Tour & Transfer to Gorakhpur

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Lumbini for sightseeing. Since private vehicles are not permitted inside the garden, a rickshaw will be hired for touring the sacred sites. Later, continue the journey to Gorakhpur, where you will visit the Gorakhnath Temple before transferring to the airport for your return journey.
