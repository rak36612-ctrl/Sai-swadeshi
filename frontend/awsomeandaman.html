Itinerary

▼

Day - 1:- Bangalore

  • • Morning: Arrive at Port Blair’s Veer Savarkar International Airport from Bangalore. • Afternoon: Check into your hotel and refresh. • Late Afternoon: Visit Corbyn’s Cove Beach, a serene spot ideal for swimming and sunbathing, located approximately 3 kilometers from the city center. Enroute, observe historical remnants such as Japanese bunkers. The nearby Snake Island is notable for scuba diving opportunities. • Evening: Dine at the beachside restaurant and beer bar operated by the Tourism Department, offering delightful cuisine in a tranquil ambiance. • Night: Return to the hotel for an overnight stay in Port Blair.
▼

Day - 2:- Journey to Havelock Island

  • • Early Morning: After breakfast, check out from the hotel and board the ferry to Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep). • Late Morning: Upon arrival, proceed directly to Kalapathar Beach, known for its adventure water sports and picturesque views. • Afternoon: Check into your hotel and enjoy lunch. • Evening: Visit Radhanagar Beach, acclaimed as one of Asia’s most beautiful beaches and renowned for its breathtaking sunset views. • Night: Return to the resort for dinner and an overnight stay in Havelock.
▼

Day - 3:- Exploring Havelock’s Marine Wonders

  • • Morning: Post-breakfast, head to Elephant Beach, celebrated for its vibrant coral reefs and clear waters. Engage in activities such as snorkeling, sea walking, or glass-bottom boat rides. • Afternoon: Relax on the beach or explore local markets. • Evening: Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Havelock .
▼

Day - 4:-Port Blair

  • After breakfast, checkout and transfer to leisure for shopping, post lunch and then proceed to Port Blair airport for your return journey to Bangalore, with the sweet memories of the tour
